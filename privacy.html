Privacy Policy for Medisure Health Clinics

Last updated: June 07, 2026

Medisure Health Clinics our operates the Medisure Health Clinics mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our appointment booking services:

  • Patient Name: To identify and confirm appointments.
  • Mobile Number: For contact purposes regarding appointments.
  • Appointment Date & Time: To schedule and manage clinic availability.

How We Use Information

The information collected is strictly used to:

  • Book and manage patient appointments.
  • Allow doctors to view and manage their daily schedule.
  • Send push notifications to doctors regarding new appointments.
  • Improve service reliability through Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics.

Data Retention

Appointment records past their scheduled date are automatically removed from our active database. Doctor accounts and schedules remain until explicitly deleted by the user or administrator.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
